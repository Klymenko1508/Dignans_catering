Dignans CAfe

Welcome to Dignans, where every meal is a celebration of flavor and tradition. Our cozy brasserie offers a perfect blend of classic dishes and modern twists, all made with the freshest ingredients. Come experience exceptional food, great company, and an atmosphere that feels like home.

MENUS

Here at First Class Catering we have a great range of menus suitable for any occasion. We are also more than happy to tailor our offering to suit any individual requirements or budget. Please don't hesitate to ask!

Delicious food for all your parties

We take pride in the quality of our food and make everything from scratch in our very own kitchen. No matter the size of the job we will ensure the food is the best available. From soup and sandwich buffets to formal sit down dinners, the quality will be evident to the finest detail.
